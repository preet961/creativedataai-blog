üß† Behind the Technology

Generative AI is powered by models like:

  • Transformers: Key architecture behind GPT and BERT.
  • GANs: Two networks (generator and discriminator) to produce realistic content.
  • Diffusion Models: Used in modern image generation tools.

These architectures allow AI to create meaningful, high-quality, and contextually relevant outputs based on learned patterns from data.
